The colour of the background changes as follows: the red constituent changes from 00 to FF between 0 and 23 hours, and the green and blue ones change from 00 to FF between 0 and 59 minutes and seconds, respectively.
The colours in the text change from FF to 00, making the colour of the text the opposite of the one of the background. Exception -- the periods of time between 11:27 and 11:32 and between 12:27 and 12:32, when seconds show between 20 and 40. At those times, the colours of the text and of the background are barely distinguishable; so, the colour of the text has been made orange.
